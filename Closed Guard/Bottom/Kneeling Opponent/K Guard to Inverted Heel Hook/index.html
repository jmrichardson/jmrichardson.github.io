    K Guard to Inverted Heel Hook

    • Drill this if just starting
    • You need to try to lift his hips off the mat so that you can reach under the leg. Try to attack the arm bar or hand fight and pull knees to the chest. Make sure to frame when he lifts his hips
    • If he grabs your head and arm to crush you, make sure you pummel your head. If you can’t pummel or kick him away, try to off balance him over your hips
    • Shrimp hips to get knee to the inside. You can open your guard and put your foot on the mat to hip out
    • Right hook goes to inside or outside armpit
    • Pull the knee on top of your chest while pushing them away. Make sure you keep the grip tight on his leg.
    • If his leg is past the knee line you can extend your back to bring it back in. Or, you can start sitting up with your arm underneath his hook to force him down and catch the heel
    • Ideally shoot the leg all the way through to the far leg

     

    • If he rolls keep your foot inside on his stomach, not out in 50/50. Then transition to 90/10
