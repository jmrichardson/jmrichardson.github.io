    Tripod sweep

    Technique¶

    • From De La Riva Guard, kuzushi by pushing with hamstring and counter pull of ankle
    • After he steps back, put DLR hook on his hip
    • Lift his lead foot up while pushing on his hip
    • Make sure you pull with butterfly hook to prevent him from back stepping
    • Grab your opponents leg to climb up
    • Bring DLR hook inside to build your base

    Notes¶

    • Always start with Kuzushi
    • If you manage to push him back with your hamstring and he stays there, then you can go for this move
    • If you miss the DLR sweep, it brings you back in which you can use to go to another attack
    • This move is low percentage
